Welcome to Decentralized Voting:
Elections made secure & simple.


Voting has never been easier! Built entirely as a smart contract on the Ethereum Blockchain, DeVote takes E-Government a thousand steps forward. Each citizen has a unique voting address with one public and one heavily encrypted private key. On top of that on election day the voters undergo a series of authentication processes to prove their identity. With DeVote YOU control YOUR future from the comfort of your living room or anywhere via tablet/phone.
