Why DeVote?

Over the past couple of years two things have been growing rapidly - distrust in mainstream politics and blockchain technology. Although not connected, these two preconditions have given birth to a need for change in the core of the electoral systems around the world. We firmly believe that DeVote meets all requirements for a transparent voting process.

You can test the application without running any additional software or even creating an ethereum wallet.
