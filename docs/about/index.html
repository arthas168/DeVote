Why DeVote?

Over the past couple of years two things have been growing rapidly - distrust in mainstream politics and the rise of blockchain technology. Although not connected, these two preconditions have given birth to a need for change in the core of the electoral systems around the world. We firmly believe that DeVote meets all requirements for a transparent voting process. In it's current 1.0 version it only supports a two-candidate election. Updates are coming soon.

Try It Out!

You can test the application without running any additional software or even creating an ethereum wallet. We've created a simple javascript demo that looks and acts exactly like the real smart contract app! It's basically a simulation of what the 2016 US presidential elections would look like if they were held on DeVote. (Keep in mind that the timer is set for 20 seconds for demo purposes, in real life it would be active for 2 days) Give it a try here.
